Description

This course covers the fundamentals of robotics, automation, artificial intelligence, and mechanical design principles.

Robotics is a rapidly evolving field that integrates engineering, programming, and AI to create intelligent machines capable of performing complex tasks. This course provides in-depth knowledge of robotic systems, including sensors, actuators, control algorithms, and real-world applications in industries such as healthcare, manufacturing, and space exploration. Learn how to design, build, and program robots while exploring the future of automation and intelligent systems.

What You will Learn?

Understand the fundamentals of robotics, design and program intelligent machines, integrate sensors and actuators, and explore real-world applications in automation and AI-driven systems.
