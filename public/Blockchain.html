Description

This course covers blockchain basics, cryptocurrencies, Ethereum, smart contracts, and dApp development. Blockchain is a decentralized and immutable digital ledger technology that records transactions across a network of computers in a secure, transparent, and verifiable manner. It operates on the principle of distributed consensus, where transactions are validated and added to the blockchain through cryptographic algorithms, ensuring data integrity and preventing tampering.

What You will Learn?

Understand blockchain consensus mechanisms, create and deploy smart contracts, build decentralized applications, and explore use cases across industries. .
